What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) when you visit them. They contain information such as your preferences, login details, and browsing history

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device when you visit them. They contain information such as your preferences, login details, and browsing history. This data helps websites recognize you and personalize your experience, making it more tailored to your needs.

Types of Cookies We Use

  1. Essential Cookies:These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly, enabling you to navigate and use its features.
  2. Analytical/Performance Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website, allowing us to improve its performance and user experience.
  3. Functionality Cookies:These cookies remember your preferences and settings, providing a more personalized experience.
  4. Targeting/Advertising Cookies:These cookies track your browsing habits and interests, enabling us to display targeted advertisements that are relevant to you.

You can manage and control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to delete or block cookies, and you can find instructions for managing cookies in your browser's help documentation.
